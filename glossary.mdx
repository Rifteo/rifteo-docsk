If you come across a term in these docs you're not familiar with, check here first. These definitions are written to be clear for non-technical readers.

## ASM (Attack Surface Management)

The ongoing process of finding, tracking, and monitoring all the digital assets (websites, servers, apps, etc.) that could be a way in for an attacker — so you know what's exposed at all times, not just during a one-time check.

## CVSS (Common Vulnerability Scoring System)

A standard scoring system used to rate how serious a security vulnerability is, usually on a scale from 0 to 10. The higher the score, the more urgent it is to fix.

## Finding

A security issue discovered during an audit or scan — for example, an outdated piece of software, a misconfigured setting, or an exposed system. Each finding is a specific problem you can act on.

## Organisation Admin

The role given to someone who manages your organisation's Rifteo workspace. Admins can view everything Members can, plus manage the asset inventory, run scans, generate reports, and manage the team.

## Organisation Member

The standard role for someone in your organisation using Rifteo. Members can view audit results, findings, and reports, but can't manage team members or account settings.

## Pentest (Penetration Test)

A simulated cyberattack carried out by security professionals to find weaknesses in your systems before real attackers do — essentially, an authorized "practice attack" to test your defenses.

## Playbook

A predefined template that outlines the steps and approach used to carry out a specific type of audit — it keeps the process consistent every time it's run.

## PTES (Penetration Testing Execution Standard)

A widely recognized set of guidelines that defines the standard steps a penetration test should follow, from planning through to reporting, so results are consistent and thorough.

## ROE (Rules of Engagement)

The agreed-upon rules that define what's allowed and off-limits during a security test — for example, which systems can be tested, when, and how — agreed on between your organisation and the team performing the audit.

## Severity

A rating that shows how serious a finding is, so you know what to prioritize. Rifteo uses five levels: **Critical**, **High**, **Medium**, **Low**, and **Info** — with Critical being the most urgent.
