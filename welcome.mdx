## What is Rifteo?

Rifteo is a security audit and attack surface management platform that helps organisations understand, track, and act on their security posture. Instead of scattering audit results across spreadsheets and emails, Rifteo brings your findings, asset inventory, and remediation workflow into a single dashboard that your whole team can use.

At the core of Rifteo is **Teo**, your AI assistant. Teo reviews your audit results and attack surface data to surface relevant recommendations, helping you prioritize what matters most without needing deep security expertise to interpret raw findings.

Rifteo is built for organisations that want continuous visibility into their security posture — not just a one-time report, but an ongoing view of assets, findings, and how they change over time.

## User Roles

Rifteo defines two roles for organisation users. This guide covers both.

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-------------------|
| **Organisation Admin** | Full access to the organisation's Rifteo workspace: view audit results and findings, manage the asset inventory, activate and schedule Attack Surface Management scans, generate reports, manage team members, and configure account/notification settings. |
| **Organisation Member** | View audit results, findings, and the asset inventory; view Attack Surface Management scan results; view and export reports. Cannot manage team members or account-level settings. |

<Note>
Auditor and platform Admin roles are internal roles used to produce and manage audits, and are outside the scope of this guide. This documentation is written for **Organisation Admins** and **Organisation Members** only.
</Note>

## How to use this guide

Use the path below that matches your role:

**If you're an Organisation Admin:**
1. Start with [Your Role](/your-role) to understand what you're responsible for.
2. Review [Your Audit Results](/results) and the [Findings Dashboard](/findings-dashboard) to see your current posture.
3. Set up [Attack Surface Management](/what-is-asm) scans for ongoing monitoring.
4. Explore [Teo AI recommendations](/teo-recommendations) to prioritize remediation.
5. Configure your [team and account settings](/team-management) as needed.

**If you're an Organisation Member:**
1. Start with [Your Role](/your-role) to understand what you can access.
2. Review [Your Audit Results](/results) and the [Findings Dashboard](/findings-dashboard).
3. Check the [Asset Inventory](/asset-inventory) to see what's covered.
4. Use [Reports](/generating-reports) to export findings for stakeholders.

If you run into unfamiliar terms along the way, the [Glossary](/glossary) has you covered.
