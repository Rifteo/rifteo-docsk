## How to reach support

If you've gone through the docs and still need help, here's how to reach us:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Email support" icon="envelope">
    Send us a message at [support@rifteo.fr](mailto:support@rifteo.fr). This is the fastest way to reach the team for account or technical issues.
  </Card>
  <Card title="In-app support" icon="comment">
    From your Rifteo dashboard, use the **Contact Support** option in your account settings to open a ticket directly — this keeps your request tied to your organisation's account for faster troubleshooting.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
If your organisation has a dedicated Slack Connect channel with the Rifteo team, you can also reach out there directly for quicker back-and-forth on active issues.
</Note>

## Response times

| Request type | Expected response time |
|---------------|------------------------|
| General questions | Within 1 business day |
| Account or billing issues | Within 1 business day |
| Technical issues affecting scans or reports | Within 4 business hours |
| Critical/urgent issues (e.g. inability to access your account) | As soon as possible, prioritized over other requests |

Response times apply during business hours (Monday–Friday). Requests submitted outside business hours are addressed on the next business day.

## Common reasons to contact support

You don't need to have a complex problem to reach out — here are some of the most common reasons people contact us:

- **Account access issues** — trouble logging in, resetting your password, or accessing your organisation's workspace.
- **Team management** — adding or removing Organisation Members, changing roles, or transferring Organisation Admin ownership.
- **Scan or scheduling issues** — an Attack Surface Management scan didn't run as expected, or you need help adjusting a schedule.
- **Findings or report questions** — you're unsure how to interpret a finding, or need help exporting a report in a specific format.
- **Billing and subscription** — questions about your plan, invoices, or upgrading/downgrading your subscription.
- **General feedback** — suggestions, bug reports, or anything else you'd like the Rifteo team to know about.

For anything not listed here, reach out anyway — we're happy to help, or point you in the right direction.
